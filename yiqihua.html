F

F was a strategic program. In the F program, there were all the functions and products needed to build a banking APP, from which our small and medium-sized bank clients could choose and assemble an APP that met their own needs.

At that time, the backstage product shelves were all financial products, which were relatively single. Clients wanted to enrich the product shelves by providing cost-effective non-financial products, to meet the diversified consumption scenarios of users. After discussion among stakeholders, it was decided to increase the sales of some popular commodities in the application by integrating peripheral resources.

But compared to the purchase of the financial product, the biggest difference was that the various options for choosing, the logistics information for adding and editing and the installment payment method needed to be considered when users bought the entity goods. Therefore, we needed to increase these considerations into the transaction process and a new credit payment with installment function into the original payment system. We hoped this credit payment own its independent name and brand for multi-channel extension in the future, it could give users perceived advantages and credibility. After the team discussion, we named it "Yiqihua", so I decided to use the Chinese character "èŠ±" combining with shopping bag as elements to design a logo, which would convey the meaning of these keywords like convenience, freedom, high credit, low rate to make users have a pleasant feeling when they shop on our app using Yiqihua Credit Pay.
