F

F was a strategic program. In F program, there were all the functions and products needed to build a banking APP, from which our small and medium-sized bank clients could choose and assemble an APP that met their own needs.

At that time, the backstage product shelves were all financial products, which were relatively single. Clients wanted to enrich the product shelves by providing cost-effective non-financial products, so as to meet the diversified consumption scenarios of users. After discussion among stakeholders, it was decided to increase the sales of some popular commodities in the application by integrating peripheral resources.

But compared to the purchase of financial product, the biggest difference were that the various options for choosing, the logistics information for adding and editing and installment payment method needed be considered when users bought the entity goods. Therefore, we needed to increase these consideration into transaction process and a new credit payment with installment function into original payment system. We hoped this credit payment own it's independent name and brand for multi-channel extension in future, it could give users perceived advantages and credibility.
