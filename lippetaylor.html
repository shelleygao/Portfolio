Web Redesign

My client said:
“ We want to remake our entire website (www.lippetaylor.com) from scratch. Our current site has too much copy, too many pages, and overall too much content. We want a more streamlined (minimalist) website and UX experience. ”

What I found:
After browsing the website, I found:
● The original site is colorful, but no obvious brand attributes.
● The work section is not highlighted enough, which is important to demonstrate the strength of the organization and to build trust.
● The information structure of the home page is not particularly clear, so that customers can not understand the core information in a short time.
● Legibility is not taken into account in the presentation of content.

What I have done:
Therefore, in view of the above points and the customer's wish, I made the following improvements:
● Primary and secondary color definitions: enhance brand recognition and personality.
● Navigation disclosure: makes navigation more direct.
● One page of vertical scrolling: browse all sections on one page.
● Color and size contrast: highlights visual hierarchy.
● The use of the grid layout: diversified layout according to the primary and secondary content, Improve legibility.
The interaction effects includes:
● Tab toggle effect
● Parallax effect
● Hover reflection
